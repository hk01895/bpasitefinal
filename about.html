Our Music

Our sound is a mix of hard rock with bluesy undertones, drawing inspiration from classic rock bands while exploring new territories. Our debut album, "Shadows of Sound," was released in 2023 and received rave reviews for its emotional depth and dynamic tracks. We are currently working on our second album, which promises to push our musical boundaries even further. Stay tuned for new releases!

How We Got Started

Stage Fright was formed when the members met at a local music venue in 2020. What began as a jam session quickly turned into something special. We bonded over our shared love for rock music and our desire to create something that resonated with listeners. From those first notes, we knew we had something unique. After months of hard work and countless rehearsals, we were ready to hit the stage and share our music with the world.

Who We Are

Stage Fright is an energetic rock band that blends classic sounds with modern influences. Formed in 2020, we’ve played venues across the country and quickly built a loyal fanbase with our electrifying performances and unforgettable songs. Our music speaks to those who crave raw emotion and powerful, anthemic melodies.
